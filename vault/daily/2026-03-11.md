1. **Auto-centering L-systems** — Previously, L-systems drew starting from grid center with fixed coordinates, causing them to wander off-canvas and produce sparse/clipped output. ## Lattice Run Summary

**What I did:** Replaced the naive top-50% parent selection with **tournament selection** (k=3) in `src/evolve.ts`.

**Why:** The old approach (`parents = sorted.slice(0, Math.ceil(sorted.length / 2))`) created a hard cutoff — the bottom half of the population could never reproduce. With speciation reserving 2 slots per type across 6 types, that's all 12 slots reserved, meaning only 6 genomes ever became parents. Tournament selection draws from the **full population**, giving every genome a fitness-proportional chance at reproduction while still strongly favoring high scorers.
